ABOUT THE ORGANIZER

INTEGRATED CYCLING FEDERATION OF THE PHILIPPINES (PHILCYCLING)

The Local Organizing Committee (LOC) for the Asian Track Championships 2026 is established to oversee the comprehensive planning, coordination, and execution of the championships, ensuring a world-class sporting event that meets international standards.

The event is proudly hosted by the City Government of Tagaytay, whose strong leadership and support underscore its commitment to sports development, excellence, and international cooperation. The Tagaytay City LGU plays a central role in providing strategic direction, local coordination, and essential services to successfully stage the championships.

Working in close collaboration with the host city are the Philippine Olympic Committee (POC), the Philippine Sports Commission (PSC), and the Integrated Cycling Federation of the Philippines (PhilCycling). These national sports institutions bring together their expertise in athlete welfare, sports governance, technical regulation, and event management to ensure full compliance with UCI standards.
