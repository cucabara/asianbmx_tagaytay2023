33rd Founding Anniversary of ACC & Asian Track Championships

INTRODUCTION

This useful information document has been prepared to assist the National Cycling Federations and invited guests in their preparation for the 33rd Founding Anniversary of ACC.

This documentation provides logistical and organizational details to participate in the events, including dates, venue, accommodation, transportation, accreditation, arrival, and departure arrangements.

The National Cycling Federation and invited guests are kindly requested to carefully take note of the information contained herein to ensure smooth coordination.
